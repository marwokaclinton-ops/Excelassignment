This Power BI project provides a deep-dive analysis into the sales performance of a multi-channel electronics retailer. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, focusing on product profitability, regional performance, and customer purchasing patterns.

The primary objective is to help stakeholders identify high-performing product lines (e.g., Laptops, Smartphones) and optimize inventory levels based on seasonal demand.
Key Features
Sales Overview: High-level KPIs showing Total Revenue, Total Units Sold, and Average Order Value (AOV).

Category Analysis: Breakdown of sales by product category (Audio, Computing, Home Appliances, Mobile).

Geographical Insights: A map visualization showing sales density across different regions/cities.

Trend Analysis: A time-series chart comparing current year sales against previous year targets.

Profitability Matrix: A scatter plot identifying products with high sales volume but low-profit margins.
