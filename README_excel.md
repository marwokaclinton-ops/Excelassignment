## 📊 Part 2: Data Analysis Dashboard
The dashboard analyzes a Jumia dataset, focusing on the relationship between pricing, discounts, and customer engagement.

### 🛠️ Data Cleaning & Enrichment
Before analysis, the following steps were taken:
* **Review Correction:** Converted negative review counts to absolute positive values.
* **Rating Normalization:** Extracted numerical values from string-based ratings (e.g., "4.5 out of 5").
* **Categorization:** * **Rating Category:** Categorized as Poor (<3), Average (3-4.4), or Excellent (>4.4).
    * **Discount Category:** Classified as Low (<20%), Medium (20-40%), or High (>40%).

### 📈 Dashboard Features
* **Overview Metrics:** Total Products, Average Rating, Total Reviews, and Average Discount.
* **Top Performance Charts:** Visual identification of the Top 10 products by discount and reviews.
* **Trend Analysis:** Scatter plots illustrating the correlation between ratings and reviews.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated **Slicers** for Rating and Discount categories to allow for real-time data filtering.

## 🚀 How to Use the Dashboard
1. Open `Jumia_Product_Performance_Dashboard.xlsx` in Microsoft Excel.
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard** sheet.
3. Use the **Slicers** on the right side of the screen to filter data by rating or discount level.
4. Observe how the charts and metrics update automatically to reflect your selection.
